    Cockroaches: why they appear and how to remove them effectively

    What attracts cockroaches, quick identification, kitchen hygiene steps and when professional help is needed.

    2026-01-19

    Why cockroaches show up

    They often come via shared building shafts, from neighbors, or with deliveries. Water + food residues (sink, trash, pet food) are key attractors.

    FAQ

    Do store-bought sprays solve the problem?
    Usually only temporarily. Professional baits/gel and proper placement are more effective, especially in apartment buildings.
